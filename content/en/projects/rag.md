### Core Technologies & Contributions
Targeting real-world industrial demands, I participated in the deployment and development of Large Language Models (LLM), bridging the engineering loop from theoretical algorithms to enterprise-level products.

* **Core Engine Architecture**：Utilized the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture to build an intelligent retrieval engine from scratch for the company's private API documentation, effectively mitigating the "hallucination" issue of LLMs in vertical domains.
* **Retrieval Strategy & Vector Database Optimization**: Deeply optimized the text chunking and vector embedding processes, significantly improving the retrieval hit rate and generation accuracy for highly structured technical documents.
* **Enterprise-Level Deployment**: Completed the system integration and business alignment of LLM interfaces, validating the practical commercial value of cutting-edge AI technologies in solving internal knowledge base pain points and enhancing R&D efficiency.
