This section highlights the core research projects and engineering implementations I have participated in during my undergraduate studies. My research not only focuses on theoretical algorithm innovation but also strives to bridge the gap between theory and the real physical world, with a strong emphasis on overall system architecture and low-level code implementation.

### Project Overview

- **IoT-Driven Dynamic Simulation of Historical and Cultural Blocks**: Sub-project of a National Key Research Program. Built a high-fidelity 3D virtual simulation base highly synchronized with real-world time and weather using Unreal Engine (UE) and Blender.
- **RF-LSTM Based Soil Respiration Study in Mufu Mountain Forest Ecosystem**: Key project of the "Exploration and Conjecture Plan". Responsible for building a mechanism-deep learning coupled model, integrating ecological priors into the algorithm to enhance prediction accuracy and interpretability.
- **RAG-Based API Retrieval Engine Development**: Targeting real-world industrial deployment, developed a Large Language Model (LLM) intelligent retrieval system based on the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture.
- **SARM Vision Enhancement Framework**: Engineering realization of top-journal academic outcomes. Implemented an underwater image enhancement network based on the Python/PyTorch framework, achieving deep integration of physical priors with State Space Models (Mamba).
- **Satellite Constellation Spatial Computing and Multi-Task Scheduling Engine**: Spatial computing engine R&D. Achieved high-precision simulation of multi-satellite ground coverage and optimal scheduling of observation tasks based on spherical grid divide-and-conquer algorithms and dynamic programming.
