### Core Technologies & Contributions
I led the development of the machine learning-based ecological time-series prediction model and successfully completed the project's mid-term defense in April 2026.

* **Meteorological Data Cleaning & Baseline Unification**: Developed rigorous cleaning strategies for complex real-world observation data from the Mufu Mountain forest ecosystem. During the model simulation and comparison phase, I insisted on strictly using the 'percentage only' column data as the absolute baseline for all control groups to ensure statistical rigor and consistency.
* **Mechanism and Deep Learning Coupling**: Constructed the RF-LSTM time-series forecasting coupled model. Innovatively integrated physical and ecological mechanism parameters, such as photosynthesis and microbial metabolism, into the input side, significantly improving the prediction accuracy of respiration flux and model interpretability.
* **Algorithm Support & Deployment**: The core algorithm developed in this project directly provided strong underlying technical support for the subsequent "Zhi Tan You Fang" (Smart Carbon Solutions) project.
