### Project Overview
* Duration: 2025.3 – 2025.7
* Project Level: National Key Research Program (Jointly undertaken by Wuhan University, China University of Geosciences, and Guangdong Guodi Technology)
* Role: Sub-project Member (Lead of 3D Base Construction)
* Advisor: Prof. Nengcheng Chen

### Core Technologies & Contributions
In Embodied AI and digital twin simulation training, a highly realistic 3D physical environment is a crucial component. Recommended by my advisor, Prof. Nengcheng Chen, to join this sub-project of the National Key Research Program, I took full responsibility for the construction of the system's 3D simulation base.

* **Multi-Source Data Preprocessing & Model Conversion**: Utilized Blender for the preprocessing and format conversion of raw LiDAR point cloud data, ensuring the accuracy of complex physical spatial data.
* **High-Fidelity Virtual Environment Rendering**: Completed the construction and material optimization of the 3D block models in Unreal Engine (UE), achieving breakthroughs in scene rendering performance.
* **Real-Time Virtual-Real Mapping**: Configured UE components to maintain strict synchronization between the virtual block and real-world weather and time, providing a high-fidelity scene foundation for digital twin simulations.
